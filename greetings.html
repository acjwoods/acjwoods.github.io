Greetings: 'Bonjour' and beyond!

Greetings play an important part in French culture, so as a tourist, knowing how to greet and bid farewell to the locals is a valuable skill, and is greatly appreciated. The table below provides a range of words and phrases you can use (depending on the time of day and the circumstances), and pronunciations and translations are suppled. Tip: If you really want to brush up on your pronunciation, listen to the audio clip of each pronunciation and repeat it aloud to yourself.

       
English Word French Word Pronunciation Example
Hello Bonjour bohn-zhoor
Hi Salut sah-lew
Good evening Bonsoir bohn-swahr
Welcome Bienvenue bee-an-vuh-nu
How are you? Comment allez-vous? com-on-talay-vu
I am (not) doing great Ca va (pas) bien sa-va (pa) bee-an
Do you speak English? Parlez-vous anglais? par-lay-vu on-glay
Thank you Merci mare-see
No problem De rien de ree-an
Goodbye Au revoir oh ruh-vwahr
Good nightBonne soirée bohn swah-ray
