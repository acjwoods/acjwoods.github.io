About France

Here are some interesting and useful facts about France:

  • France is ranked as the most popular tourist destination in the world, with approximately 83 million foreign tourists visiting each year.
  • The population of France is just over 66,700,000.
  • The capital city is Paris.
  • It has the world's sixth-largest economy, in terms of nominal GDP.
  • The earliest French literature dates from the Middle Ages, although some of the most known French writers are of the 19th century: Victor Hugo (The Hunchback of Notre-Dame), Alexandre Dumas (The Three Musketeers), and Jules Verne (Twenty Thousand Leagues under the Sea).
  • French cuisine is known as the finest in the world, and popular dishes include beef bourguignon and quiche.
  • More than 400 different types of cheese have originated in France, such as Brie, Camembert and Roquefort.
