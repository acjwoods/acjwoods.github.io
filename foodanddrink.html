Ready to improve your French language abilities?

The primary aim of this website to help you learn French. Whether you are a complete beginner or had some experience with the language, this resource will aid the development of your French language abilities, guaranteed! You can use this site alongside other online resources, such as WordReference.

       
English Word French Word Pronunciation Definition
Hello Bonjour bohn-zhoor Normal greeting used all day
Hi Salut sah-lew Informal greeting used all day
Good evening Bonsoir bohn-swahr Normal greeting used in the evening
Welcome Bienvenue bee-an-vuh-nu Formally welcoming someone
How are you? Comment allez-vous? com-on-talay-vu Literal: how is it going?
I am (not) doing great Ca va (pas) bien sa-va (pa) bee-an Literal: it's (not) going well
Thank you Merci mare-see Most common way of politely thanking someone
No problem De rien de ree-an Common response to 'merci'
Goodbye Au revoir oh ruh-vwahr Normally used to express well wishes when parting ways
Good nightBonne soirée bohn swah-ray Used at the end of a conversation in the evening
