Food and Drink: Feeling hungry?

From tasty tarts to rustic rolls and baguettes, France is known for its gastronomic delights, so a trip to an authentic French restaurant is a must when travelling to France, or even to another French-speaking country. This topic will provide you with some vocabulary you can use when ordering food and drink. Whether you're in a café or a traditional brasserie, those in your company will certainly be impressed!
