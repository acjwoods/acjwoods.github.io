Welcome to Apprendrew!

The primary aim of this website to help you learn French. Whether you are a complete beginner or had some experience with the language, this resource will aid the development of your French language abilities, guaranteed! You can use this site alongside other online resources, such as WordReference.

The first step

If you're ready to gain some useful vocabulary and phrases, your first decision is which topic you'd like to work on. Greetings, going out, holidays, sports and relationships are all popular starting points, and will expand your lexicon is relation to everysay subjects.

Navigating the site

Getting around this site is a piece of cake. You can familiarise yourself with all the topics, and whenever you're stuck, you can come straight back to the homepage!
