Welcome to Apprendrew!

Whether you're heading for an Alpine adventure, a Parisian paradise, or some solitude in the sun, Apprendrew will help you get clued up on some of the most useful French phrases. The primary aim of this website to help you learn French. Whether you are a complete beginner or had some experience with the language, this resource will aid the development of your French language abilities, guaranteed! You can use this site alongside other online resources, such as WordReference.

The Structure of Apprendrew

This course is structured so that you can work through the vocabulary pages at your own pace, and pick up as many new words as you can. The four primary topics available to you are:

  • Greetings
  • Directions
  • Places
  • Food and Drink

When you believe that you are ready to test yourself, you can progress on to the final quiz, which contains various question types based on videos, audio clips and images. However, if you'd rather test yourself halfway through the course, there is an interactive exercise available to test yourself on 'Greetings' and 'Directions'.

Navigating the site

Getting around this site is a piece of cake. You can familiarise yourself with all the topics, and whenever you're stuck, you can come straight back to the homepage!
