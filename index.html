Bienvenue! Welcome to Apprendrew!

Fancy learning some French? Whether you're heading for an Alpine adventure, a Parisian paradise, or some solitude in the sun, Apprendrew will help you get clued up on some of the most useful French phrases. Whether you are a complete beginner or had some experience with the language, this resource will aid the development of your French language abilities, guaranteed!

The Structure of Apprendrew

This course is structured so that you can work through the vocabulary pages at your own pace, and pick up as many new words as you can. The four primary topics available to you are:

  • Greetings
  • Directions
  • Places
  • Food and Drink

When you believe that you are ready to test yourself, you can progress on to the final quiz, which contains various question types based on videos, audio clips and images. However, if you'd rather test yourself halfway through the course, there is an interactive exercise available following the 'Directions' topic.

Additional Resources

Apprendrew isn't just a language learning tool, but it also contains plenty of information about destinations in France, as well as links to other online resources. If you'd rather bypass the French course, you can access all the additional information by either clicking here, or on the 'Useful Information' button at the top of this page.

Having trouble?

While care has been taken to ensure that a clear definition and pronunciation is given for all the new vocabulary, there may be a couple of words you don't quite understand. WordReference is a fantastic online resource you can use to double check vocabulary, or look for a synonym, and also see a word or phrase in context.
